Rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors

The rules are straightforward:

  1. Rock beats Scissors: Rock crushes or blunts scissors.
  2. Scissors beats Paper: Scissors cuts paper.
  3. Paper beats Rock: Paper covers rock.

These rules form a cyclic relationship where each option beats one and loses to another, creating a balanced game. When both players choose the same option, it results in a tie. The game is often played as a hand game between two people but can also be simulated with computer opponents or integrated into various games and applications.
